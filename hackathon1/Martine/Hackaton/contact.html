NATURAL. BEAUTY. AMPLIFIED.

MBeauty is a natural, effective, and simple solution to nail care that focuses on the maintenance, health, and longevity of nails. We apply the same anti-aging principles you use for your face, to your nails and cuticles and aim to prolong work done by your nail tech, by caring for cuticles in between appointments. Think anti-aging, but for your nails!

MEET THE FOUNDER & CEO

MELANIE BERTRAND

Founder and CEO Mélanie Bertrand is a beauty enthusiast who plans on making her mark in the nail care and beauty industry with a line of restorative nail care products.


The MBeauty nail and cuticle oil is the first natural and effective solution to nail care in a line of many more to come!


MBeauty represents a fresh perspective in the industry because it applies anti-aging principles to nail care.


For Mélanie, beauty is her escape, her self-care, her creative fuel, and her expression! Melanie love for this industry is the ethos of MBeauty and what drives this brand.
